    About

    The San Diego Latino Film Festival was started by a group of students 19 years ago. Since then, the San Diego Latino Film festival has grown to one of the largest Latino film festivals in the country.

    Over the years, countless celebrity appearances have been made to the San Diego Latina Film Festival, some of which include Eduardo Verastegui, Ximena Sarinana, Barbara Mori and many more.

    Within the past few years, over 225,000 people and 3,500 screenings have taken place. As a result, this demonstrates the growing interest in Latin film festivals.
