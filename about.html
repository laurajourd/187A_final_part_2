About The Site

This website was for the purpose of bringing together a collection of information about the popular cultural film festivals in San Diego. Upon conducting research on San Diego film festivals, we began to notice that there was no single resource to find information about all the film festivals that happen. Rather, we noticed that all the festivals are decentralized in their own niche.

As a result, we decided to approach this project and conduct user-based interviews to find out what is relevant to attendees of the local San Diego film festivals. Upon collecting interview data, we received a lot of feedback from our informants about the highly distributed, and inconsistent information that is availible on the web. This website is an attempt to organize information, and provide film festival patrons a single resource for all festivals.

Who We Are And Where We Came From

This task was prompted by Dr. David Kirsch of the Cognitive Science Department at UC San Diego for the final project in a course in multimedia design.

The three collaborators of this site are Rachel Cusing, from California, Laura Jordain from Bordeaux, France and Ren Nakanote from San Diego, California.
