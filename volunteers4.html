    All volunteers should report to the Volunteer Check-in booth for the festival, located at UltraStar Mission Valley Cinemas (7510 Hazard Center Dr, San Diego, CA 92108), unless you are volunteering in our offices (2921 El Cajon Blvd, San Diego, CA 92104) or at a Gala.

    Festival Volunteer Drivers


    The 19th Annual San Diego Latino Film Festival, March 8-18, 2012, is looking for volunteer drivers to help transport special guests to and from airports, the train station, hotels, the theater and various events during their stay in San Diego.
