    Interested in entering into this year's Jewish film festival?


    The deadline for this year's San Diego Jewish Film Festival is September 15, 2012!

    Entry Information

    • No entry fee for the SDJFF.
    • Series: Narrative, Documentary, Animation, Experimental, and Children's Films with Jewish content.
    • Films 30-minutes or less will be considered short-subject.
    • Screeners of foreign language films must be subtitled in English
    • Acceptable formats: 35mm, Beta DVD NTSC and PAL formats accepted, 1/2" VHS NTSC (PAL also accepted, but not preferred)

    More information about submission

    How to Submit

    Ship to:
    San Diego Jewish Film Festival
    Lawrence Family JCC
    4126 Executive Drive
    La Jolla, CA 92037-1338

    Also Include:

    • Print source: name, address, phone, e-mail, and website address
    • Director, country of origin, year, running time, language(s), and format the print is available on for festival screenings (BetaSP, DigiBeta or 35MM)
    • Synopsis of film
    • Filmmaker's contact information and bio (if available)
    • Any publicity which may include: press reviews, awards, award nominations, premier dates and city, and/or other relevant press materials.

    Send electronically to: Sandra Kraus, SDJFF Producer

    • At least one image from the film. Please send in HIGH resolution (300 DPI or higher) for print
    • A director photo
