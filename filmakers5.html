    Interested in entering in to the San Diego Film Festival?


    The San Diego Film Festival is accepting submissions NOW in the following catagories for September 26-30th 2012:

    • "Fresh Features" - From wild road trip comedies to heart-felt dramas, this catagory consists of a wide variety of different narrative works
    • "Reel Life Docs" - A vivid reflection of the world beyond our doors - taking you places you might never see, and introducing you to people you will be glad you got to know.
    • "Film Rocks Music Videos" - Musically-inspired short films and music videos to celebrate the symbiotic relationship between two powerful art forms.
    • "Local Love - San Diego Showcase" - A series showcasing the talent of SD filmmakers and films primarily shot here.
    • Green Screen - Environmental Series - A series of docs curated to raise awareness and promote social activism.

    How to Submit

    Deadlines

    • Early Deadline: February 1
    • Regular Deadline: April 1
    • Late Deadline: June 1
    • WAB Extended Deadline: June 15

    Entry Fees

    • $55 all films over 30 minutes
    • $45 all film under 30 minutes
    • $35 all San Diego residents, films shot in San Diego county, student films, and music videos. (Films can be any length.)
    • All fees increase $10 after January 1st.

    How to Submit

    1. Withoutabox
    2. Download and print out application and apply by mail
