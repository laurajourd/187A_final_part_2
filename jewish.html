    About

    The San Diego Jewish Film festival is brought to you by the San Diego Center for Jewish Culture. In February of 2012, they celebrated their 22nd annual San Diego film festival.

    Their primary goal is to educate and illuminate through evocative, independent fiction and documentary films that portray the Jewish experience from current to historic global perspectives.
