    About

    The San Diego Italian Film Festival is a non-profit 501c(3) organization which provides films and media around Italian culture.

    All films are open to the public, and films which are in Italian are sure to include English subtitles. Film are screened in a vareity of different venues, so be sure to check out the events calendar on the their website.

    Donate

    Your donations help to give the San Diego Italian Film Festival the means to keep bringing these great stories to our city, to produce special events celebrating Italian culture, and to continue to expand the piazza. The San Diego Italian Film Festival is a non-profit 501 c (3) organization, therefore your donation is tax-deductible.
