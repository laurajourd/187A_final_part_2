    Sponsorship Opportunities Information


    The San Diego Film Festival (SDFF) is THE destination for film; a launch pad for creative filmmakers where urban edge and perfect-weather escapism meet to bring the best of independent cinema to San Diego's progressive community.

    This year, SDFF is strengthening our reputation as the premier event for hot films, cool parties and nothing but the best hospitality. Want to place your product in front of the eyes of the movers and shakers of Southern California? Through on-site displays, on-screen advertisements, and on-brand promotions, SDFF will create an awe-inspiring plan of action and ideal placement for your product or brand.

    Contact: sponsors@sdff.org for more information.
