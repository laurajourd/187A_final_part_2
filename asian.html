    About

    The San Diego Asian Film Foundation (SDAFF) is one of the largest media arts organizations in North America that focuses on Asian American and Asian international cinema.

    Our mission is to connect and transform audiences with the human experience through the Pan Asian media arts.

    Our values are: Innovation/Creativity, Excellence, Leadership, Creating Community and Fun/Hip/Cool.

    General Information:

    - Online

    The best way to purchase tickets for this festival is to go through the festival website (not yet available). Discounts are available for Foundation members and groups of 10 or more.

    - Box Office

    Theater box office sales begin much later than online sales. The box office offers discounts for students, seniors, and military. Tickets for free events must be picked up at the box office.


    Important Policies:

    No refunds. Exchanges made only for oversold programs.

    Any open seats 15 minutes before showtime are made available to the public, on a first-come first-served basis. For sold out shows, a rush line is formed outside the theater 30 minutes before showtime.
