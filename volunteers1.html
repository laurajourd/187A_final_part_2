    Volunteers Opportunities Information


    One of the best ways to get involved with us is to volunteer. In fact, many members of our current staff began as volunteers and grew into their jobs! We are always seeking talented, dependable volunteers in areas of:

    Fundraising / Graphic Design / Office Administration / Social Media/Marketing / Special Event Planning / Street Team


    Sign up now online to volunteer and we will update you on opportunities to volunteer as they come up.
