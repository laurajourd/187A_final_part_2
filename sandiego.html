    About

    The San Diego Film Festival is San Diego's premier "red carpet ready" film festival.

    Upon celebrating their 10 year anniversary in 2011, they have become an asset to the community, hosting their festival in the heart of the Gaslamp District in downtown San Diego.

    With a crew of over 300 filmmakers and actors, the San Diego Film Festival features a wide array of diverse and award winning films.

    Donate

    The San Diego Film Foundation (SDFF), an educational 501(c) 3 non-profit organization that produces the annual San Diego Film Festival, aims to educate, enlighten and entertain, bringing greater exposure and appreciate of independent film.

    Through your generous donations, we will be able to continue to provide San Diego with an exceptional outlet for independent film; and we can come together as a community to celebrate the art and power of independent film. Any amount will help, and all donations are tax-deductible.
